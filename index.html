NFMe(Non-Fungible Me) ID are your Data Avatars of the Metaverse. Join the Data DEX and complete a "seed profiler task" and have your very own NFMe ID minted and stored in your wallet. NFMe ID's have "Personal Data Categories" (PDC) linked to them; these feed data into the NFMe ID Avatars.

‍

Apps built on Itheum's "Data Collection and Analytics Toolkit" feed data into the PDCs. Itheum's "Personal Data Adaptors" can also discover and harvest on-chain and off-chain personal data and lock it inside your "Data Vault" and link it to your NFMe ID.

‍

As more data is added to the NFMe ID; it's "data signature" changes and more "accessories", "evolution traits" and "skins" are made available to your NFMe ID. This is akin to purchasing gaming accessories and traits to augment your in-game NFT characters. Your NFMe ID is organic and evolves like a human as more data is added to it.

Your data is truly "non-fungible" as it's unique to you, and Itheum's Data NFTs are a perfect tokenized medium for you to trade your data in primary and secondary data markets. Slice out certain segments of your data from your NFMe (e.g. Data from a specific app or category) and mint them into Data NFTs and have them in your wallet - ready to be traded.

‍

Limit the distribution of your highly sensitive or protected datasets to a smaller amount (similar to limited edition NFTs we see in the market today) or choose to implement royalties on your most sensitive data (e.g. your DNA profile). Data NFTs have a generative art wrapper tied to the unique signature of the data and this makes it appealing to buyers of these unique assets.
